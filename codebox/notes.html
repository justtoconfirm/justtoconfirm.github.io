Wiggle is a European online sports retailer, selling cycle, run, swim and outdoor equipment and apparel. It is based in Portsmouth, England. The company is part of the WiggleCRC group, which also includes Chain Reaction Cycles

Wiggle CRC Group now employs over 1,000 staff across offices in Portsmouth & Belfast.

Over 100 cycle sportives, runs and obstacle course races.

In 2013, Wiggle became the title sponsor of the UCI ladies professional cycling team, Wiggle Honda.
