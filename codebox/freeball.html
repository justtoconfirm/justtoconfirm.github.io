Freeball

  1. No one owner can control a team and must be owned by fans or fan groups.
  2. Predicted results are generated first and actual results generated second. A goal is scored if the value of the predicted result matches with the actual result in the same index (e.g. 1-1-1 / 1-0-1 = 2 goals).
  3. Every team receives a vote to determine league, competition or rule changes. Each team must vote 0 (No) or 1 (Yes). If 50% of the votes or above are marked as 1 (Yes) then the vote stands. The number of teams in the league will determine how many votes are available.
